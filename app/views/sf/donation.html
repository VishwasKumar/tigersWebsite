How Do We Allocate The Funds?

Guiding Principle

We will be following our core values while making grants and fellowships. The foundation believes in transparency, accountability and accessibility. We would fund grants to organisations that are innovative and follow a bottom-up approach. We are accountable to our grantees, funders, donors and the communities we work with. We will hold regular consultations with these groups. The grant making process will be transparent and accessible. 

Grant Making Body

Decisions on grantees (individuals and organisations) will be made by a panel, which will meet twice a year. This body would consist of both individuals who have experience of working with the communities and those who are experienced in the area of grant making. Due processes will be followed while making the decisions in keeping with our policy.
