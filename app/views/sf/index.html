Who are We?

TMM is a youth organization which is popularly known as Tiger Mane Machas.The members of this organization i.e the Tigers are found typically on Saturday and/or Sunday afternoons playing in BMS College of engineering. Tigers spend most of their time on weekdays slogging for different corporate company's while the weekends are spent playing FIFA or football. TMM do sign new tigers every year based on the performance and dedication of the candidates. After signing a player for TMM player will be monitored on punctuality and determination for 6 months after which he will be handled a Tiger FC Badge by the president and senior most Tiger "Shishira Haleneer". Keep roaring Tigers !!
