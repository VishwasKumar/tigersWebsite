As an individual who wants to contribute to the society, to make a difference, to be the change; you can help us. We welcome any contribution, big or small, from individual donors. We like to engage with a range of individuals from all walks of life; students, government employees, corporate employees, retired persons etc. Aside from giving us donations, you can volunteer or intern with us, connect us to your friends and provide us with resources as well.

How Can You Help?

Donate to us

1

You as an individual can contribute to our cause and can associate with us. You could also donate a small amount regularly through payroll giving. This way, you will be associated with us over a long term and there would be greater impact of your contribution. One-off small amounts too matter to us. As a small foundation these contributions mean much to us and for you it will mean that the support actually reaches the grassroots level groups and makes a difference.

Volunteer/Intern with us

2

Anybody, students, professionals, Indians, foreigners,. can volunteer or intern at our organisation or with organisations we support. You can learn and teach, interact with experts and can gain field experience. You will get a peek into the lives of people quite different from you. A chance to learn and grow. We make an effort to structure a customised volunteering and interning schedule in keeping with unique, ideas and qualities, which helps both our collectives, and you.

Connect us to your friends

3

Besides your involvement you may also know a friend, a group, a corporate body or an organisation that you would like to link us to. Please do that. Now. Enlarging the circle of supporters and friends is important to our work. More people are discussing the issues that we are working with is such a positive step. Especially there are so few open conversations around issues of sexuality.

Provide Resources

4

You can also provide us with resources such as your time, expertise, office equipment, food stuff etc. You could also mobilise resources â€“ old newspapers, basic medicines etc and hand them over to us. The community is always in need of various resources. You can also link us to others, your family, friends, colleagues etc. who can provide resources or can engage with us.
